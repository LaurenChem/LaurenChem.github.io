FAQs

When is the RSVP deadline?
Please confirm your attendance by Sunday, August 31st at the latest. This will allow us to ensure you're catered for.


What should we wear?
The event dress code is cocktail. Please be aware that the pathway to the ceremony location is uneven, so consider avoiding footwear such as stiletto heels.


Do you have a registry?
The gift of your time, support and love is the best present we could possibly receive on our wedding day <3. However, if you would like to give a gift, we will have a wishing well at our reception.


How do we get there?
There is plenty of parking space, should you wish to drive. This is also an easy dropoff/pickup point for taxis or rideshare. Please allow 5min for the short walk to the ceremony space. The Gap bus station is a ~15min walk if you prefer public transport.


Can I bring someone else along?
All guests will be invited by name (including children). Please refrain from bringing any additional people, as we simply will not have the space (or food!) to accommodate them. Please contact us if there is any confusion.


I have a dietary requirement, will it be catered for?
Yes, of course! Please include any dietary requirements in your RSVP, and contact us as soon as possible if your situation changes after this time. Food will be served banquet style, with gluten and dairy free options provided. Any additional allergies or restrictions will be catered for individually.


Can I take photos?
We will be having an unplugged ceremony - please keep your phones switched off and put away. We will have both a photographer and videographer to capture everything! You're welcome to take photos outside of this (i.e. cocktail hour and reception), but we'd really appreciate if you wait for us to share on social media first!


Who do I contact if I have any questions on the day?
Not us :)
Lauren Edwards (MOH), Cervantes Feng (BM), Geoff Blackwell (Dad 1) or Erwin Schrantz (Dad A) are the best contacts on the day.
